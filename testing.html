  • Poison Ivy's Proposal To Harley Quinn Was The Sweetest In Comics

    Long-time fans of Harley Quinn and Poison Ivy have shipped the couple ever since the two first started expressing romantic interest during Batman: The Animated Series. In more modern times, the two have become one of DC's most popular couples. Bringing fan predictions to life, the two officially tied the knot in the Injustice: Year Zero, cementing them as the perfect friends-to-lovers progression. Fans may have seen the marriage coming, but none of them were ready for such a sweet proposal from the typically harsh Poison Ivy. The two have always aimed to stand out in their actions, and they may have risen above the rest as far as romance for this moment.
